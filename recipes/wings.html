Buffalo Wings

Description

A sentimental one. Brings back memories of countless skipped classes in highschool with my friends during happy hours in one of the first american-style restaurants in Warsaw. This recipe is quite easy, but attention to detail is key (just as in coding).

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken wings
  • teaspoon of baking powder
  • teaspoon of salt
  • teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Frank's Red Hot Sauce or similar
  • butter
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Cut wings in half to separate the drums from the wingettes. Pat the wings dry with a paper towel. Preheat the oven and line a rimmed baking sheet with foil
  2. Combine baking powder, salt, and garlic powder
  3. Place the chicken wings in a large mixing bowl and toss them in the dry seasoning until well-coated. Assemble the wings in a single layer over the rack
  4. Bake in the center of the oven for 50 minutes, flipping halfway
  5. In a medium bowl, stir together melted butter, sugar, and hot sauce
  6. Transfer chicken wings to a bowl, and toss, drizzle with sauce and toss to coat. Serve with your favorite sauce
