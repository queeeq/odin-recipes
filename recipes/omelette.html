French Omelette

Description

A beautiful breakfast dish which allow you to experience how sometimes simplicity is the perfect answer. One of the best ways to make eggs - both structure-wise and taste-wise. Takes some training to achieve perfection, but it is definitely worth learning. Just one ingredient made with love.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • tablespoon of butter
  • salt to taste
  • chives (optional)

Steps

  1. whisk the eggs in a bowl and add salt
  2. start melting the butter in a pan on a low heat
  3. add the eggs and mix them rapidly until the eggs are shiny, but not runny
  4. fold the omelette using spatula one part a time, then flip it gently on a plate
