Queeeq Steak

Description

First dish that I've learned to cook after moving out from my family home. A treat for meat-lovers. Ribeye is an amazing cut of beef, one of the most flavorful out there. There is no need for special or fancy ingredients, just steaks, seasonings, a brush of oil, and a pat of butter for perfection.

Ingredients

  • boneless ribeye steak
  • tablespoon of butter
  • good quality salt and pepper
  • garlic
  • fresh rosemary (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to medium heat
  2. Grill steak 2-4 minutes per side for medium-rare (depending on steak's thickness)
  3. Add butter, garlic and rosemary after the first flip, bathe the steak in it, tilting the pan
  4. Remove everything from the pan, let the steak rest for 5-10 minutes
